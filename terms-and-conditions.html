Tribal App - Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: 16 April 2025

1. Purpose of the App

Tribal is designed to digitally preserve tribal culture, rituals, languages, traditional songs, and heritage. The platform aims to:

  • Share authentic community knowledge across generations
  • Provide educational and cultural insights to a broader audience
  • Support the recognition and upliftment of tribal identity

All features of the App are intended to respect and celebrate indigenous traditions.

2. Eligibility

The Tribal app is open to all users—regardless of caste, religion, or background—who wish to engage respectfully with tribal knowledge. Users may be asked tribal-related questions during login to promote awareness, not to restrict access.

Harmful, disrespectful, or inappropriate behavior may result in restricted or terminated access without notice.

3. Content Collection and Cultural Data

Content is derived from oral traditions passed down through generations in Adivasi communities, including songs, stories, and rituals. All content is collected with cultural respect and is used solely for educational and preservation purposes.

4. Content Usage

All content is curated by the Tribal team or verified contributors. Users cannot directly upload or publish content. Each item is credited appropriately. Sensitive topics are shared to promote understanding, not to cause harm.

5. Reproduction and Publishing Policy

No content may be reproduced or distributed externally without written permission. Requests should be emailed to: trblppspprt@gmail.com.

Request Format (Mandatory):

  • Your Full Name
  • Permanent Address
  • Contact Number
  • Email ID
  • Title of the Article
  • Author Name (as credited in the app)
  • Purpose of Publication
  • Intent & Impact of Publication

6. Community Guidelines

Users cannot publicly post content. Suggestions can be made via the Suggestions section. Inappropriate suggestions may lead to account suspension.

7. Privacy and Data
